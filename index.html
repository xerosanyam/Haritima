 Haritima | हरितिमा       The First Environment Related Fest to be orgainsed in Uttarakhand!                                                            Event Details have been uploaded !

First Environmental Fest. It'll capture your mind.

First Environmental Fest to be organised in Uttarakhand Region! It will be one of its kind college festival related to only Environment.
It is a 4 day event and has events that will be totally Eco-friendly, and will play a very important part in the motivation of the students towards their duties to nature.

For the Youth. By the Youth.

The fest aims at empowering the young to know about the environment they live in and motivate them towards its conservation.
